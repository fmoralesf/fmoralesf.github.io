About

Background
Francisco Morales is an Assistant Professor at Universidad de los Andes, Chile. He holds a Ph.D. in Business Administration from the University of Colorado, Boulder. Francisco's research looks at the intersection between innovation and strategic human capital. He studies how firms can benefit from managing their talent and employee collaboration in a global environment.
Francisco's research has been presented at several international conferences and published in prestigious management academic journals. His research has been funded by the Chilean National Agency of Research and Development (ANID).
Francisco teaches management courses at the undergraduate and graduate levels. He has taught courses in the United States and Chile.
Research Interests
Innovation and strategic human capital, talent management, employee collaboration in global environments, organizational performance, and strategic management.
